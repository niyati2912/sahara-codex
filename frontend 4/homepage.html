Your Real-Time Human Safety Net

SAHARA connects people in need with trusted, verified helpers nearby. From roadside issues and medical support to mental health check-ins and everyday kindness, SAHARA routes the right help to the right person at the right time.

About SAHARA

SAHARA (Safe And Helpful Aid & Response Alliance) is a community-powered platform inspired by the idea that technology should amplify trust, not replace it. We partner with campuses, neighborhoods, nonprofits, and local agencies to build a reliable safety net that is fast, respectful, and transparent.

SOS Alert System

Firebase notifications • Google Maps API

One tap triggers a geo-fenced alert to responders within range. The incident card includes live location, contact preference, and safety notes. Updates appear in a shared timeline so everyone stays aligned until the case is closed.

Real-Time Helper Matching

Medical • Mental Health • Pharmacy • Legal • First Aid

Our matching engine weighs proximity, skill tags, language, and availability to connect users with the most relevant helper. Automatic hand-offs keep support continuous if a responder becomes unavailable.

Volunteer Verification

College IDs • KYC • NGO/Institutional Credentials

To safeguard users, helpers complete identity checks and code-of-conduct training. Tiered verification and visible trust badges set clear expectations and accountability.

How It Works

Clear steps from request to resolution. Every action is logged for transparency and post-incident learning.

1) Request Help

Users tap SOS or choose a category (medical, roadside, personal safety, wellness). They can share live location and optional notes, or request discreet/anonymous support.

  • Quick presets for frequent needs
  • Multilingual prompts & voice input

2) Match & Dispatch

The engine notifies nearby verified helpers and routes the case to the best fit. Helpers can accept, decline, or re-route to specialists.

  • ETA & route guidance via Maps
  • Two-way updates and safety check-ins

3) Resolve & Recognize

Once resolved, users rate safety and outcome. Helpers earn Karma Points, badges, and community recognition. Trends feed into local readiness planning.

  • Transparent incident timeline
  • Auto-generated thank-you notes

Safety Preparedness Tips

Simple habits make the biggest difference during uncertain moments. Share these with family, roommates, and neighbors.

At Home

  • Keep a first-aid kit, flashlight, and spare batteries in a visible location.
  • Maintain a small stash of drinking water and dry snacks.
  • Post emergency numbers on the fridge and in your phone favorites.
  • Save the SAHARA SOS widget on your home screen.

On the Move

  • Share your trip ETA with a trusted contact.
  • Carry essential medications and a power bank.
  • Avoid isolated routes late at night; request a virtual walk-along if needed.
  • Know your local 24/7 pharmacy and hospital locations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are helpers verified? ▾

Helpers submit identity proofs and institutional credentials and complete training. Levels of verification are visible on their profile along with incident history.

Can I request help anonymously? ▾

Yes. You can hide your personal details and use chat-only mode. We still log safety outcomes without exposing identity.

What if the situation escalates? ▾

One tap upgrades an incident to “priority”, alerts additional responders, and offers to call 112. Helpers are trained to step back when professionals take over.

How do Karma Points work? ▾

Points accrue for verified actions and learning modules. Moderators can issue badges; organizations can sponsor rewards and letters of recommendation.
